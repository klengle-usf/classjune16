# Midnight on the Windowsill: How a Small Orange Cat Named Marmalade Learned That Every Shadow Holds a Secret Worth Chasing

Marmalade was a small orange cat who lived above a bakery and spent his evenings watching the alley from the windowsill. One night he noticed a shadow that moved without him — Silhouette, a cat made of darkness who danced across the brick wall every dusk. A tabby named Biscuit told him not to worry; Silhouette had never caught anything, but she always came back. Marmalade sat still, chirped once, and learned that some shadows are not meant to be caught — they are meant to be followed, just far enough to remind you the world is bigger than the sunny spot on the floor at two in the afternoon.
